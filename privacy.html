Privacy Policy

Avalon Audio Assistant

Last Updated: November 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Avalon Audio Assistant ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to improve the app experience:

  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous usage data including game selection, feature usage, and session completion rates. This data is collected locally and used for app improvement.
  • App Preferences: Your voice settings (language, volume, speed, source) are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit personal identifiers such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or device identifiers.

How We Use Information

The limited data we collect is used solely for:

  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Understanding feature usage patterns
  • Identifying and fixing bugs
  • No data is shared with third parties or used for advertising purposes

Data Storage

All user preferences and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain external servers or cloud storage for user data. All game content, audio files, and configuration data are bundled with the app and stored locally.

Third-Party Services

The app may use system-provided Text-to-Speech (TTS) services on your device. These services are provided by Apple and are subject to Apple's privacy policies. We do not transmit any text or audio data to external TTS services.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the app's feedback feature or via the App Store listing.
