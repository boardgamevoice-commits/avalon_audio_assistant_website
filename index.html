Board Game Audio Assistant

iOS Voice Assistant for Board Games

About

Board Game Audio Assistant is an iOS app designed to help players and hosts quickly get into board games like Avalon, One Night Ultimate Werewolf, and Werewolf. The app provides bilingual voice guidance, process management, and auxiliary tools to enhance your gaming experience.

Features

  • Bilingual voice guidance (Simplified Chinese / English)
  • Rule explanation mode for new players
  • Full process hosting with step-by-step voice instructions
  • Interactive judge mode for special scenarios
  • Built-in tools: random player selector, timer, dice roller, scoreboard
  • Customizable voice settings (volume, speed, source)

Supported Games

Avalon One Night Ultimate Werewolf Werewolf (Standard)

Platform

iOS - Requires iOS 16.0 or later
Available on iPhone and iPad
